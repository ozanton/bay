## Determination of prospective tariff for telecom company
***Statistical Data Analysis***

### Brief description of the project

The customers are offered two tariff plans: "Smart" and "Ultra". It is required to find out which plan is the most profitable. The client is provided with a large data sample consisting of records for 500 users with information about their activity.

### Used libraries
*pandas*, *numpy*, *math*, *matplotlib*, *scipy*

### Description of work performed
Performed data preprocessing:
- Check for gaps;
- Checking for duplicates;
- Matching data types.  
Performed merging of tables.  
Conducted exploratory data analysis - analyzed user activity.  
Hypothesis testing was performed - the left hypothesis and the alternative hypothesis about the dependence of revenue from tariffs and the user's region of residence were put forward. The hypotheses were tested using Student's test.
